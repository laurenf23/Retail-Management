About Lauren Fallon Designs

A fictional brand created for class to explore what a small online accessories shop might look like.

Lauren Fallon Designs imagines a collection of everyday accessories made for students, young professionals, and anyone who wants to add a little more intention to their outfits without being overly formal.

The brand concept focuses on slow fashion: smaller batches rather than mass production, classic shapes instead of fast trends, and materials that are meant to last beyond a single season.

This site was built using HTML and GitHub Pages for a retail management assignment. Hand-coding the pages provides more control over layout, colors, and typography while keeping the design simple and easy to navigate.
