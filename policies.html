Policies

This is a sample policy page for a fictional course project. Policies are written in a clear, customer friendly format.

Shipping

Orders are typically processed within 1 to 3 business days.

  • Standard shipping estimated 3 to 7 business days after processing
  • Tracking provided when available
  • Shipping rates shown at checkout

Returns and Exchanges

If something is not quite right, we want to make it easy to resolve.

  • Returns accepted within 14 days of delivery
  • Items must be unused and in original condition
  • Exchanges available for the same item when possible

Order Changes

If you need to update an address or cancel an order, contact us as soon as possible.

  • Order changes are only possible before the order ships
  • Once shipped, we cannot guarantee edits to delivery details

Product Care

Simple care tips help pieces last longer and stay looking their best.

  • Store jewelry away from water and fragrance
  • Spot clean fabric items when possible
  • Keep accessories out of direct heat and sunlight for long periods

Privacy

Customer information is used only to fulfill orders and provide support.

  • No selling of personal information
  • Email used for order updates and customer service
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails anytime

Customer Support

Questions are always welcome. We aim to respond quickly.

  • Typical response time 1 to 2 business days
  • Contact page includes email and a simple message form
