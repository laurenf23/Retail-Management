Designed for Everyday Wear

Lauren Fallon Designs is a fictional brand created for a course project. The idea behind the shop is to offer accessories that feel comfortable, wearable, and special without being overly formal.

Each imagined piece reflects slow fashion values: smaller batches, thoughtful materials, and designs that can stay in a wardrobe for more than one season.
